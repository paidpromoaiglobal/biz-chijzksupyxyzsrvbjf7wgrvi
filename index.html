About Us

Dr. M Dental Clinic & Implant Centre, led by the highly skilled Dr. Mudassir Mohiuddin, is a trusted name in comprehensive dental care in New Mallepally, Hyderabad. With an outstanding 4.9-star rating from over 142 satisfied patients, our clinic is renowned for combining advanced techniques with a warm, patient-first approach. Whether you need a routine check-up or a complex dental implant procedure, our team ensures every visit is comfortable, transparent, and effective.

We specialize in a full range of dental services including dental implants (Straumann), root canal treatments, laser gum therapy, zirconia crowns, and much more. Open every day of the week from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, we make quality dental care accessible and convenient for families across Hyderabad. Our friendly staff, modern equipment, and Dr. Mohiuddin's hands-on expertise have made us the go-to dental destination for patients of all ages.
